Tasks

You will be given 3 types of tasks to complete during this experimental evaluation. Each task has specific instructions on what is expected of you. Please aim to complete each quickly and accurately and press the "submit" button once you are done. Please read these carefully and clear up any questions you have with your instructor before you begin the task.

Align on Rectangle Task

This task requires you to line up each letter/number on the black rectangle in the center of the screen. Your goal is that each letter/number object lies perfectly on the rectangle. Orientation on the rectangle doesn't matter.

Dodecahedron Tasks

A dodecahedron is a 10 sided shape, where each face is a pentagon. This task has a dodecahedron in the center of the screen. The dodecahedron has "cutouts" that correspond to 3 different numbers/letters. As you can see in the image below, there is a purple dodecahedron, with a grey cut-out 9. This corresponds to the pink 9 on the top left. All 3 of the numbers/letters corresponding to the cut-outs are visible in your scene. This task requires you to slot each letter/number into the dodecahedron.

Arrange Scene Tasks

These tasks have 2 sub-tasks.
First, you must place the table onto the tiled floor so that it is centered on the highlighted rectangle.
Second, you are required to place the lamp onto the table in the center of highlighted circle. Ensure that both the table, floor and lamp all have the same rotation.
